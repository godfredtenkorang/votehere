Breaking the cycles of poverty - Tips on how to make and save money

Trending

9th July, 2025

By Benjamin

1. Saving money is harder when you are poor and that’s not your fault. It’s not that you’re undisciplined. It’s that your income is too low You can’t save what doesn’t exist. So the problem isn’t just saving , it’s earning more. 2. You don’t save money by cutting out just small expenses.The little expenses aren’t the problem it’s the big ones. Rent, lifestyle, loans, and habits.Focus on fixing the real leaks, not feeling guilty over small joys.
