Policy

When a consumer emails us, we collect their email address, access and visitation history, and any optional information they choose to submit (such as survey responses and/or site registrations). We don't sell or share the information we collect with outside parties; instead, we use it to improve the caliber of our services and the information on our websites.

Information Gathering and Usage

In order to help us provide the services to you, we may ask for information about you during the registration process, including your name, company name, email address, and billing address (collectively, "Registration Information"). Additionally, in order to use our service for nominee processes like election notification, voting eligibility verification, and vote results, you will be asked to share information about your nominees (collectively, "Nominee Information"), including email addresses, phone numbers, and mailing addresses. VoteAfric NEVER DISCLOSE TO ANY THIRD PARTY VOTER OR REGISTRATION INFORMATION.

Cookies

A cookie is a small piece of information that is transferred from the computers running a website to your browser and saved on the hard drive of your computer. It frequently contains an anonymous unique identifier. VoteAfric requires cookies in order to function. We don't use permanent cookies; instead, we use them to store information about the current session. To safeguard you against someone unintentionally accessing the contents of your account, you must log in to your account after a predetermined amount of inactivity has passed.

Analytics

We use non-personally identifiable data about site visitors, their use of our website, and their visits to other categories of websites across the internet, using Google Analytics (including Display Advertising and Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting). We use this data to enhance and personalize our marketing campaigns and website. Check out Google's Privacy Policy to find out how they use the information they gather. You can always change your Ad Settings on Google and visit Google to opt out of Google Analytics Display Advertising on all websites.

Data Storage

Hardware, software, networking, storage, and other related technologies are provided by third-party vendors and hosting partners on behalf of VoteAfric. You maintain all ownership rights to your data even though VoteAfric owns the databases, code, and application rights.

Disclosure

In certain situations, like when you violate the terms of service or need to comply with a subpoena, VoteAfric may disclose personally identifiable information. Information sharing may be required to look into, stop, or take action regarding suspected or confirmed illegal activity. Examples of such activities include fraud and circumstances where there may be a risk to someone's physical safety, among other things. Sharing information may also be necessary as required by law.

Changes

VoteAfric may revise this policy from time to time. If there are any substantial changes to the way we handle personal information, we'll let you know by posting a notice on our website or sending a notice to the primary email address you provided when creating your account.
